# Week 1 Day 1: Ready-to-use Examples

## Three FAIR mini scenarios (quick teaching)

1. **Findable scenario**
   - A dataset is uploaded as a file, but there is no DOI and no descriptive metadata.
   - Discussion prompt: Which FAIR principle is weakened first, and why?

2. **Accessible scenario**
   - A dataset download link works today, but there is no access statement and the metadata page is removed after a short period.
   - Discussion prompt: How does FAIR interpret “accessible even if restricted”?

3. **Reusable scenario**
   - A dataset includes measurements, but units, variable definitions, and the license are missing.
   - Discussion prompt: Which part of Reusable is missing?

## Three short discussion prompts

1. What is one piece of documentation you wish you had when you reused a dataset?
2. Can a dataset be “accessible” without being “open”?
3. Which FAIR principle do you personally care about most and why?
